Ham and Cheese Melt

Woah, that looks good! Here's how to make it:

-- Ingredients --

Make sure not to go shopping for these items while you're hungry. Trust me, you end up with a whole 'nother grocery cart of stuff you dont need

  • 2 slices whole grain bread
  • 2 teaspoons butter
  • 2 slices Swiss cheese - (although I usually go for cheddar, but whatever grills your sandwich)
  • 2 thin slices deli ham
  • 1 teaspoon mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon whole grain mustard
  • 1 cherry on top - (not really, that would be kind of gross)

-- Directions --

Now that we've got all of the ingredients, let's make this sandwich happen!

  1. Preheat a skillet over medium-high heat

  2. Spread one side of each slice of bread with 1 teaspoon butter

  3. Place once slice butter-side down in the hot skillet. Top with Swiss cheese and ham. Spread the unbuttered side of second slice of bread with mayonnaise and mustard; place it butter-side up on sandwich

    4. -- (Personally, I would add mayo to the other side too, but your sandwich, your life) --

  4. Cook in the hot skillet until golden brown and cheese is melted, about 3 minutes per side. Try not to scroll on insta too much or it'll go from golden brown to outer-space black.

  5. Plop it on a plate, wait a little bit, and enjoy!
